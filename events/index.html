Discover and connect with new interesting people around you!

If you are an event organizer, be it a conference, a concert, or a meetup, we can make your event better. Glancee can engage your audience by promoting conversations. It can increase the visibility of your staff and sponsors by connecting them with your crowd. It can help you better understand your event with unique analytics.

Engage your audience

Glancee is a social utility that brings people together by finding the important connections they share. With instantmessaging and VoIP your attendees will engage in new conversations and build important relationships that will last over time, adding value to your event even long after it is over.

Support your sponsors and staff

Staff members and sponsors can be listed as featured users to appear on top of the list and be easily recognized. Featured users can broadcast important information and receive live feedback. In this way, sponsors can get more visibility and directly engage with people interested in their services.

Understand your crowd

Our engine also provides detailed insights in real-time about the people that are attending your event and the connections they are forming. Who are your most popular attendees? Where do they work? What do they do? What do they like? Our spatial analytics answer these questions without compromising their privacy.
