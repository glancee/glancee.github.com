Why Glancee?

Glancee is a social utility that brings people together by finding the important connections they share. During occasions like conferences, concerts, and sport games Glancee can help event planners create more engaging experiences for attendees and sponsors.

For Attendees

With Glancee, attendees will be able to easily find others who share common friends and similar interests. Glancee makes it easy to connect through instant messaging, voice-over-ip calls, and email. This helps people create new, meaningful connections and participate in more interesting conversations, making your event that much more valuable.

For Sponsors

Staff members and your sponsors can be listed in Glancee as featured users. This makes them appear at the top of our main screen, where we list the most interesting people near the current user. Moreover, if the sponsors will use Glancee too, they will be able to easily discover those in the crowd that are more interested in their services.

Understand your crowd

Finally, we make it easy to get detailed insights of the type of people that are attending your event. Where do they come from? What is their level of education? What are they interested in? How do they interact with other attendees? Our data engine can answer these questions through the analysis of aggregate data, without compromising our users' privacy.

Getting started

To use Glancee for your event, we just need the name, website, time, and place of the event. When Glancee users arrive at the event, they will be greeted by a welcome message to your event and they will be asked to check-in. Once in, they will be able to connect with your featured sponsors and other attendees. If you are interested in finding out more, fill out the form below.
